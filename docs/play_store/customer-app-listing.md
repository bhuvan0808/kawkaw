## Keywords / ASO targeting

Google Play has **no dedicated keyword field**. Ranking is driven by the **app title, short description, and full description**, plus install velocity and ratings. Weave the terms below naturally into those three fields (avoid keyword stuffing — Play penalizes repetition).

### Primary target terms (highest intent)
| Term | Weave into |
|------|-----------|
| grocery delivery | Title, Short desc, Full desc (heading + intro) |
| medicine / pharmacy delivery | Short desc, Full desc (Pharmacy section) |
| food delivery | Short desc, Full desc (Food section) |
| parcel delivery / courier | Full desc (Parcel section) |
| Bhadrachalam delivery | Title-adjacent, Short desc, Full desc intro + closing |
| cash on delivery | Short desc, Full desc (repeated, it's a differentiator) |
| online order tracking | Full desc (Live tracking section) |

### Secondary / long-tail terms
- "order groceries online Bhadrachalam"
- "medicine home delivery Telangana"
- "local delivery app"
- "hyperlocal delivery"
- "doorstep delivery"
- "near me grocery store delivery"
- "prescription upload pharmacy"
- "send parcel near me"

### Placement guidance
- **Title** carries the most ASO weight — keep "Grocery & Delivery" in it.
- **Short description** is indexed and shown high — fit "grocery, pharmacy, food, parcel" + "Bhadrachalam" + "cash on delivery".
- **Full description** — use the section headings (SHOP GROCERIES, ORDER FROM PHARMACIES, GET FOOD DELIVERED, SEND PARCELS) as natural keyword anchors. Mention "Bhadrachalam" 2–3 times max.
- Do **not** use competitor brand names (Play policy violation) or unrelated trending keywords.

## Content rating guidance

Complete the **IARC content rating questionnaire** in Play Console (App content → Content rating). Expected answers for this app:

- App type: **Reference, News, or Educational / Utility, Productivity, Communication, or Other** → choose **"Other" / shopping utility**. (Not a game.)
- Violence, sexual content, profanity, controlled substances, gambling: **No** to all.
- **Pharmacy nuance:** the app lets users order regulated medicines via a licensed pharmacy with prescription verification. This is *commerce*, not drug-reference content — answer the "references to drugs" question honestly: the app **facilitates purchase of legal pharmacy products with prescription controls**, it does not promote or instruct drug use. Expected resulting rating: **Everyone / PEGI 3 / IARC 3+**.
- User-generated content / social features: **No** (no chat, no public posts).
- Shares user location: **Yes** (for delivery) — declared separately in Data safety, not the rating.

**Target audience & content (App content section):** target age group **18+** is recommended because orders involve a financial transaction (cash on delivery) and pharmacy purchases; at minimum select adult/older audiences, **not** "children". The app is **not** designed for or directed at children, so **Families / Designed for Families = No**.
