## Plain-language summary

Kaw Kaw (operated by **KawKawTech Pvt Ltd**) accepts **Cash on Delivery (COD) only**. Because we do not collect card or bank details, refunds are handled as a **cash adjustment at delivery** or as **store credit** to your Kaw Kaw account.

- If an item is **missing, damaged, wrong, or not delivered**, you may be eligible for a refund/adjustment.
- Report issues **promptly** — ideally at the doorstep or within the window below.
- **Perishable, food, and pharmacy** items have limited eligibility for safety and hygiene reasons.
- Cancellations before dispatch generally incur no charge; later cancellations may not be refundable.

This summary is for convenience only. The full policy below governs.

---

## 1. COD context — how refunds work

Kaw Kaw is **COD-only** in this version and does **not** collect or store card, UPI, or bank-account details. As a result, refunds are issued in one of the following ways:

- **Cash adjustment at delivery** — for example, if an item is missing or rejected at the doorstep, the rider deducts the corresponding amount from the COD total so you pay only for what you accept.
- **Store credit** — a credit applied to your Kaw Kaw account for use on a future order, where a doorstep cash adjustment is not possible.
- **Cash reimbursement** — where required and where a doorstep adjustment was not possible, arranged through support; the method and timeline are confirmed when your request is approved.

The applicable method depends on the situation and is confirmed when your request is reviewed.

---

## 2. Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund, adjustment, or store credit in cases such as:

- **Non-delivery** — a confirmed order that was not delivered (and where the failure is not attributable to you, e.g. unreachable/incorrect address or refusal to pay).
- **Missing items** — items billed but not delivered.
- **Wrong items** — items materially different from what you ordered.
- **Damaged or defective items** — items that arrive damaged, spoiled, or unfit, where this is not due to your handling after delivery.
- **Quality issues** reported within the applicable window with reasonable evidence (e.g. photos).

We may request reasonable proof (photos, order details) and may decline requests that appear fraudulent, abusive, or outside this policy.

---

## 3. How to request a refund and the window

- **At the doorstep:** Inspect your order on delivery where possible. For missing, wrong, or visibly damaged items, raise it **with the rider at the time of delivery** so a cash adjustment can be made before you pay.
- **After delivery:** Contact **support@kawkaw.in** (or use in-app support) **within [REFUND REQUEST WINDOW, e.g. 24–48 hours] of delivery**, with your order details and a description/photos of the issue.
- We will review and respond within the timelines in Section 6. If approved, we apply the cash adjustment, store credit, or reimbursement as appropriate.

Requests made after the applicable window may not be eligible, except where required by applicable consumer law.

---

## 4. Pharmacy and perishable exceptions

For safety, hygiene, and legal reasons, the following have **limited or no return/refund eligibility** once delivered, except where the item was damaged, wrong, or defective on arrival:

- **Prescription medicines and other pharmacy items** — generally **not returnable** once delivered, given handling and regulatory requirements. Issues such as wrong or damaged pharmacy items reported promptly will be reviewed.
- **Perishable goods and prepared food** — limited eligibility; report quality issues immediately on delivery with evidence.
- **Opened, used, or tampered items** where the issue is not a pre-existing defect.

Where a pharmacy order is **rejected at verification** (invalid/missing prescription), no charge applies because the order is not dispatched.

---

## 5. Cancellation refunds

- **Before dispatch / preparation:** You may cancel through the app; since payment is COD, no money changes hands and **no charge applies**.
- **After dispatch but before delivery:** Cancellation may not be possible or may incur a charge (for example, for prepared food, perishables, or a rider already en route). Any applicable adjustment is handled as store credit or per Section 1.
- **Pharmacy:** Once a prescription is verified and the item dispensed/dispatched, cancellation may not be possible.
- **Kaw Kaw-initiated cancellations** (e.g. item unavailable, outside serviceable area, failed verification) result in no charge to you for the cancelled portion.

See the [Delivery Policy](./delivery-policy.md) for failed-delivery handling and the [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md) for cancellation rights.
