# Kaw Kaw — Delivery Policy

## Plain-language summary

Kaw Kaw (operated by **KawKawTech Pvt Ltd**) delivers **Grocery, Pharmacy, Food, and Parcel** orders within **Bhadrachalam, Telangana, India**.

- We deliver within the **serviceable area** shown in the app at checkout.
- A **delivery fee** in ₹ may apply, with **free delivery above a configurable order value**; the exact fee is shown before you confirm.
- We currently accept **Cash on Delivery (COD) only** — please keep **exact change** ready.
- Estimated delivery times are shown in the app and are estimates, not guarantees.
- Accurate address and availability help us deliver on time; failed deliveries may be re-attempted or returned.

This summary is for convenience only. The full policy below governs.

---

## 1. Serviceable area

- Kaw Kaw currently delivers within **Bhadrachalam, Telangana, India**, and adjacent zones as configured in the app.
- Serviceability is determined at checkout based on your delivery address and location. If your address falls outside the serviceable area, you will not be able to place the order.
- We may expand, reduce, or temporarily pause the serviceable area (for example, during adverse weather, festivals, or operational constraints) without prior notice.

---

## 2. Delivery hours

- Deliveries are available during the **operating hours** shown in the app, which may vary by category (**Grocery, Pharmacy, Food, Parcel**) and by store/partner availability.
- Orders placed outside operating hours may be scheduled for the next available window or rejected.
- Hours may change on public holidays, during emergencies, or for operational reasons.

---

## 3. Delivery fees

- A **delivery fee in Indian Rupees (₹)** may apply to each order. The exact fee is **shown at checkout before you confirm**.
- **Free delivery** may apply for orders **above a configurable threshold** (for example, "free delivery above ₹[THRESHOLD]"). The applicable fee and any free-delivery threshold are **configurable by Kaw Kaw** and displayed at checkout.
- Additional charges (such as small-order handling, surge, or distance-based fees) may apply where shown.
- All fees are payable in cash on delivery together with the order amount.

---

## 4. Estimated delivery times

- The app displays an **estimated delivery time** based on factors such as distance, route (computed using the OpenStreetMap/OSRM stack), rider availability, store preparation time, and current demand.
- Estimated times are **good-faith estimates, not guarantees**. Actual times may vary due to traffic, weather, address issues, store delays, or other factors.

---

## 5. Cash on Delivery (COD) handling

- Kaw Kaw is **COD-only** in this version. Pay the rider in **cash** for the **full confirmed amount** (items + applicable delivery/other fees) on delivery.
- **Please keep exact change ready.** Riders may not always be able to provide change for large notes. If exact change is unavailable, any shortfall/adjustment is handled per the [Refund Policy](./refund-policy.md) (for example, as store credit).
- The rider may confirm the order contents with you at the doorstep before completing the COD transaction.
- Refusing to pay the confirmed COD amount for a correctly delivered order may result in account action under the [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md).

---

## 6. Address accuracy and recipient availability

- Provide a **complete, accurate delivery address** and a reachable phone number, and ensure your location/pin is correct so the rider can reach you.
- Be **available to receive** the order during the estimated window. The rider may contact you using the number associated with the order.
- Incorrect addresses, unreachable recipients, or unavailability may cause delays, failed delivery, or cancellation, and may incur charges as described below.

---

## 7. Failed delivery and re-attempts

- If the rider cannot complete delivery (for example, recipient unreachable, wrong/incomplete address, no one available to pay COD, or unsafe access), the rider will attempt to contact you and wait for a reasonable period.
- Depending on the situation, the order may be **re-attempted**, **rescheduled**, **returned**, or **cancelled**.
- For **perishable, food, and pharmacy** items, failed deliveries may not be re-attempted and may be treated as completed/forfeited where the failure is attributable to the customer, subject to the [Refund Policy](./refund-policy.md).
- Repeated failed COD deliveries attributable to the customer may lead to account restrictions.

---

## 8. Perishable, food, and pharmacy handling

- **Food and perishable goods** are handled with care, but their quality depends on timely receipt; please accept and inspect them promptly on delivery.
- **Pharmacy items** require a **valid prescription** and **pharmacist verification** before dispatch (see the [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md)). Orders failing verification will not be delivered.
- Certain items (perishables, prepared food, opened or temperature-sensitive products, and prescription medicines) are subject to limited return/refund eligibility for safety and hygiene reasons (see the [Refund Policy](./refund-policy.md)).
- Inspect items at the doorstep where possible and report missing, damaged, or incorrect items immediately (see the [Refund Policy](./refund-policy.md)).

---

## 9. Parcel deliveries

- For **Parcel** deliveries, you are responsible for ensuring the contents are lawful and properly packaged, and that both pickup and drop-off details are accurate.
- We may refuse to carry items that are illegal, hazardous, restricted, or otherwise prohibited.

---

## 10. Rider safety

- Riders may decline or delay delivery where conditions are unsafe (for example, severe weather, inaccessible or unsafe locations, or threatening behavior).
- Customers must treat riders with respect and provide safe access for delivery. Abuse, harassment, or unsafe demands toward riders are prohibited and may result in cancellation and account action under the [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md).
- Rider live location is used during active deliveries for routing, tracking, and safety, as described in the [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md).
